What is the Monthly Calling Campaign?
The Monthly Calling Campaign is an effort by Citizens' Climate Lobby to inspire as many people as possible to call their Member of Congress and express their concern about climate change.

Congressional staffers and representatives have told us nothing is more powerful in helping a representative know what their constituents want than written letters and phone calls.
What is CCL?
CCL exists to create the political will for climate solutions by enabling individual breakthroughs in the exercise of personal and political power.

You can learn more at citizensclimatelobby.org
